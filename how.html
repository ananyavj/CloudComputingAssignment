How TimeWeave Works

Scheduling across timezones is a nightmare. This tool makes it a visual breeze. Here's how to master it in 30 seconds.

1

Add your team members

Type a name and select their specific city or timezone. Don't worry about UTC offsets; we handle daylight savings and everything automatically using the browser's built-in intelligence.

2

Set their working hours

Define when each person is usually "at their desk" in their own local time (e.g., 9 AM to 5 PM). The grid will immediately update to show their availability relative to yours.

3

Read the Grid

The 24-hour grid shows everyone's relative availability.

• Purple = Working
• Green = Everyone is working at once!
• Amber ✨ = The best possible slot for your meeting duration
4

Cycle through "Best Windows"

If there are multiple times everyone is free, use the "Next Window →" button in the bottom gold bar to highlight alternative options. Change the "Meeting Duration" in settings to find longer or shorter blocks.

5

Export and Share

Once you find a perfect time, click "Export Schedule". We generated a pre-formatted summary you can paste directly into Slack or email so everyone knows when to jump on the call.
